My philosophy on sketches...

I have always struggled to keep a journal regularly and have always admired of those who could find an outlet in writing about their day, especially after a difficult one. However, through sketching—capturing even the mundane aspects of life—I discovered that the quality of my lines and brushstrokes could reflect and help me recall the day I had experienced. And so, art became my way of journaling: my favorite pastime, my meditation, my practice, and my life.
